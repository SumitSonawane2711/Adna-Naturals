## **Your 4-Step Ayurvedic Baby Hair Care Routine for Monsoon Season in India** 

**Step 1: Pre-Wash Scalp Massage (5 Minutes)** 

Use lukewarm **coconut or sesame oil** infused with neem leaves.  **Why?** Creates a protective barrier against humidity. 

**Frequency**: 2 - 3 times/week. 

**Step 2: Gentle Cleansing with Organic Baby Shampoo** 

● Wet hair with lukewarm water (never hot) 

● Use **1 - 2 pumps** of <u>**natural baby shampoo**</u>**** 

● Massage for 60 seconds, focusing on the crown 

● Rinse thoroughly, leftover residue invites fungi 

**Pro Tip**: In high-humidity cities like Kolkata, shampoo **every alternate day**. In drier Pune monsoons, **twice a week** is enough. 

**Step 3: Post-Wash Herbal Rinse** 

Make a DIY rinse: 

\- 1 cup cooled green tea 

\- 5 drops tea tree oil 

\- 1 tsp hibiscus powder 

Strain and pour over the scalp. No need to rinse out. 

**Step 4: Air-Dry + Detangle** 

● Pat dry with a soft muslin cloth 

● Use a wide-tooth wooden comb 

● Avoid hair dryers, heat damages baby follicles

## **DIY Natural Baby Shampoo Recipe (Grandma’s Monsoon Secret)** 

**Ingredients:** 

\- ½ cup liquid castile soap (unscented) 

\- ¼ cup aloe vera gel 

\- 1 tbsp reetha powder 

\- 5 drops tea tree oil 

\- 1 cup distilled water 

**Method:** 

1\. Dissolve reetha in warm water (strain) 

2\. Mix with castile soap & aloe 

3\. Add tea tree oil 

4\. Store in pump bottle (use within 2 weeks)

**Safety**: Patch test on inner arm. Avoid eyes.
