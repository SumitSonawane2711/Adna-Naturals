## **Why Baby Skin Needs Extra Protection**

#### **Thin Barrier and High Absorption**

A baby’s skin is up to 30 - 50% thinner than adult skin. That means whatever you apply including shampoo is absorbed faster and deeper.

#### **Increased Risk of Irritation**

The immature barrier means harsh cleansers can trigger dryness, itching, and micro tears in the scalp that go unnoticed until symptoms show.

***

## **Hidden Dangers in Regular Baby Shampoos**

#### **Sulfates (SLS/SLES) and What They Do**

These are the foam boosting agents that make lather. They also strip natural oils, leaving skin dry and inflamed.

#### **Parabens and Hormonal Disruption**

Parabens mimic estrogen and are linked to endocrine imbalance, a major reason pediatric dermatologists advise against them for infants.

#### **Artificial Fragrances and Allergic Reactions**

“As little as 1 - 2% of babies develop fragrance-triggered dermatitis in the first year,” report pediatric dermatology case studies.
