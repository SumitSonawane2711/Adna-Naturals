## **Detailed Step By Step Guide: How To Apply an Ayurvedic Diaper Rash Cream Properly** 

Most parents apply rash cream like it is a casual step, but technique matters more than you think. Here is the process dermatologists and Ayurvedic doctors often recommend: 

**Step 1: Clean the area gently** 

Use lukewarm water or soft cotton with a mild cleanser. Avoid vigorous wiping.&#x20;

**Step 2: Pat dry completely** 

Even a small amount of moisture can worsen rashes. Make sure folds are dry.&#x20;

**Step 3: Apply a thin even layer of the cream** 

Do not rub too hard. A thin coating of zinc and herbs works better than a thick lump.&#x20;

**Step 4: Let the skin breathe for a few minutes** 

If possible, leave the diaper off for two to five minutes.

**Step 5: Use regularly** 

Frequent but gentle use allows the herbal ingredients to build a strong protective layer. 

Many mothers have told me that this small routine made a bigger difference than switching ten different creams.
