## **Prevention Tips for Modern Moms: Keep Rash at Bay Naturally**

Prevention is key in any diaper rash treatment plan. Here's a quick checklist:

* Change diapers frequently to avoid moisture buildup.
* Allow air time: Let skin breathe diaper-free for short periods.
* Use fragrance-free wipes and switch brands if irritation persists.
* Apply barrier creams proactively with natural oils.
* Diet tweaks: For breastfed babies, mom's intake of acidic foods can influence rashes.
